Page Splicer

Splits a PDF into N equal parts, drops blank pages, then splices matching pages from each part side by side onto one wide page — page 1 of part A next to page 1 of part B, and so on.

⇅
Drop a PDF here, or click to choose one
.pdf files only — processed entirely on your device
Download merged PDF
Blank detection — a page is treated as blank if it renders to an (almost) uniformly white canvas at low resolution.
Part split — after blank removal, the remaining pages must divide evenly by the number of parts you set.
Privacy — your file never leaves this browser tab; there is no server component.
